Ingredients:
 - 1 0.25oz package active dry yeast
 - 1 tsp white sugar
 - 1 cup warm water
 - 2 1/2 cups bread flour
 - 2 tbsp olive oil
 - 1 tsp salt
 
Directions:
 1) Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. In a medium bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar
	in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
 2) Stir in flour, salt, and oil. Beat until smooth. Let rest for 5 minutes.
 3) Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round.
	Transfer crust to a lightly greased pizza pan or baker's peel dusted with cornmeal.
 4) Spread with desired toppings and bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or 
	until golden brown.
 5) Let pizza cool for 5 minutes before serving.
